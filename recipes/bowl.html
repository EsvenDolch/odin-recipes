Bowl

Description

This dish I'm going to show you is highly nutritious, and only the egg needs to be cooked.

Ingredients

  1. For the Bowl:
    • Canned tuna in water or oil
    • Ripe avocado
    • Hard-boiled eggs
    • Cherry tomatoes or chopped tomatoes
    • Red onion
    • Lemon juice
    • Extra virgin olive oil
    • Salt and black pepper

Steps for the Bowl

  1. If they are not already cooked, boil the eggs for 8-10 minutes, peel them, and cut them into quarters.
  2. Cut the avocado into cubes and the cherry tomatoes in half. If using onion, slice it very thinly.
  3. In a medium bowl, mix the drained tuna with the chopped tomato and onion.
  4. Add the avocado cubes to the bowl. Drizzle with lemon juice and olive oil. Season with salt, pepper, and chili flakes (if using). Mix everything together.
  5. Serve the tuna and avocado mixture in a bowl or on a plate. Place the hard-boiled egg quarters on top or around the mixture.
