Quesadillas

Description

This recipe is ideal for a quick lunch or dinner, using ingredients that are usually found at home..

Ingredients

  1. For the quesadillas:
    • Flour or corn tortillas
    • Refried beans
    • Grated cheese that melts well (cheddar, mozzarella, Oaxaca, etc.)
    • Butter or oil for the pan

Steps to prepare the quesadillas

  1. Place the beans in a small pot or microwave-safe container and heat until warm and easily spreadable.
  2. Spread a thin layer of warm refried beans on half of a tortilla.
  3. Sprinkle a generous amount of grated cheese over the beans.
  4. Heat a flat skillet or griddle over medium heat. Add a small amount of butter or oil. Place the folded tortilla in the skillet (make sure the filling stays inside).
  5. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, pressing lightly with a spatula, until the tortilla is golden brown and crispy and the cheese has melted completely.
  6. Cut the quesadilla in half or into pieces.
