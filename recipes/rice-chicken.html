Rice Chicken

Image by timolina on Freepik

Description

Below, I will show you how to prepare chicken with rice, which you can enjoy alone or with your family on a pleasant afternoon.

This recipe is one of my favorites because of its flavor and how easy it is to prepare. I hope you like it.

Ingredients

  1. For the chicken:
    • Chicken: Thighs, breasts, or other chicken parts
    • Onion
    • Garlic minced
    • Tomatoes
    • Chicken broth or water
    • Vegetables for sauce
    • Carrots
    • Salt
  2. For de rice:
    • Your preferred variety of rice
    • Water or broth
    • Salt
    • Oil

Steps for chicken

  1. Season the chicken pieces with salt. In a large pot or deep skillet with a little oil, sear the chicken on all sides until golden brown. Remove and set aside
  2. In the same pan, add more oil if necessary and sauté the chopped onion and garlic until translucent
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes and carrots (and any other vegetables you want to add to the sauce). Cook for a few minutes until the tomatoes soften and form a base sauce.
  4. Return the chicken to the pot. Pour in enough chicken broth or water to cover it.
  5. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat, cover, and simmer until the chicken is tender and cooked through (approximately 30-45 minutes, depending on the pieces). Adjust the salt in the sauce.

Steps for Rice

  1. Rinse the rice if desired until the water runs clear.
  2. Heat a little oil in a rice pot. Add the rice and toast it lightly (sauté) for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Add the water or broth (usually 2 parts liquid to 1 part rice; check the instructions for your variety). Add salt
  4. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover the pot tightly, and cook without uncovering for the recommended time (usually 15-20 minutes).
  5. Remove from heat, let stand covered for 5-10 minutes, then fluff the rice with a fork before serving alongside the chicken.
